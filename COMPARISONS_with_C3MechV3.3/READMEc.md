This directory contains comparisons between C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 (the published version).

Kinetics shows plots and tables of the rate constants present in both C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 that differ by more than a factor of 2 at any point in the 0.1-100 atm and 500-2000 K range.

Thermo shows plots and tables of thermochemistry data for species present in both C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 that differ by more than 2 kcal/mol in enthalpy (H) and/or gibbs free energy (G)
