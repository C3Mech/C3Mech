## Comparisons
This directory contains comparisons between C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 (the published version). The plots were generated automatically with Auto-Mech routines https://tcg.cse.anl.gov/papr/codes/automech/. 

**KINETICS**  
Plots and tables of the rate constants present in both C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 that differ by more than a factor of 2 at any point in the 0.1-100 atm and 500-2000 K range.
Files are organized by kinetic subset, in the same way as sub-modules are organized in the submodules directory. Excel files contain a summary that is easier to read, where a selection of temperatures is shown for each reaction (500, 1000, 1500, 2000 K). 
Note: reactions with bath gases other than (+M) are currently not managed by the model. For details on bath gas dependent reactions in the hydrogen module, refer to the main text of the publication.

**THERMO**  
Thermo shows plots and tables of thermochemistry data for species present in both C3MechV4.0 and C3MechV3.3 that differ by more than 2 kcal/mol in enthalpy (H) and/or gibbs free energy (G).
Files are organized by kinetic subset, in the same way as sub-modules are organized in the submodules directory. Excel files contain a summary that is easier to read, where a selection of temperatures is shown for each species (500, 1000, 1500, 2000 K).
